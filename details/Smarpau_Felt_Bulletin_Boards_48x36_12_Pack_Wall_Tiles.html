Smarpau Felt Bulletin Boards

Transform your workspace into a stylish and functional area with these Smarpau felt bulletin boards. The set includes 12 large cork board tiles that offer flexibility in arranging your space, whether you choose the standard 48x36 layout or the expanded 96x48 design. Each tile is crafted with a durable felt surface that not only adds a touch of elegance but also helps protect your walls from scratches and damage.

With included removable adhesive tabs, installation is effortless and non-permanent. This makes them perfect for temporary setups or when you need to reposition your board without any tools. Whether you're in an office, classroom, or home studio, these boards can double as a note board, memo board, or decorative wall panel.

The water-resistant and eco-friendly material ensures these cork board tiles are suitable for high-traffic areas such as kitchens, dorm rooms, or even recording studios. Plus, the sound-absorbing properties make them ideal for creating quiet spaces in nurseries and other environments where noise control matters.

Perfect for both professionals and DIY enthusiasts, these Smarpau felt bulletin boards are designed to last. With 35 rust-proof thumb tacks included, you can easily pin documents, calendars, and other essentials, making them a must-have for any workspace looking to stay organized and clutter-free.
